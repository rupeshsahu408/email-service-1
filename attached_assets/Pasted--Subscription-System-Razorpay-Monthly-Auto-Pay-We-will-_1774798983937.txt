## 💳 Subscription System (Razorpay - Monthly Auto Pay)

We will use **Razorpay Subscriptions** for Business Plan.

---

## 🎯 Plan Details

* Plan Name: Sendora Business
* Price: ₹199/month
* Billing Cycle: Monthly (auto-renew)
* Free Trial: Optional (can add later)

---

## 🔁 Subscription Flow

1. User clicks “Upgrade to Business”
2. Razorpay subscription checkout opens
3. User completes payment (card / UPI / etc.)
4. Subscription is created

---

## ✅ On First Payment Success

* Upgrade user plan → "business"
* Set:

  * `plan = business`
  * `status = active`
  * `next_billing_date = +30 days`

---

## 🔄 Auto Renewal (Important)

Every month:

* Razorpay auto charges user
* On success:

  * Extend `next_billing_date` by 30 days

---

## ❌ If Payment Fails

* Mark:

  * `status = past_due`

* Send user notification:

  * “Payment failed, please update payment method”

* After grace period (e.g. 3–5 days):

  * Downgrade to free plan

---

## 🛑 Cancel Subscription (User Control)

User must be able to cancel anytime.

### From Settings Page:

* Button: “Cancel Subscription”

On click:

* Cancel Razorpay subscription
* Keep plan active until current billing period ends

---

## ⚙️ Settings Page (Subscription UI)

Show:

* Current Plan: Business
* Status: Active
* Next Billing Date
* Price: ₹199/month

Buttons:

* Cancel Subscription
* Update Payment Method

---

## 🔔 Webhooks (VERY IMPORTANT)

Handle Razorpay events:

* `subscription.activated`
* `subscription.charged`
* `payment.failed`
* `subscription.cancelled`

Use these to:

* Update user plan
* Maintain billing accuracy

---

## 🔐 Important Rules

* User loses business features after expiry
* Golden tick removed after plan ends
* Custom domain access disabled (or limited)

---

## 💡 UX Notes

* Show message:
  “You can cancel anytime from settings”

* Show trust:
  “Secure payments powered by Razorpay”

---

End Goal:
Simple, automatic, and user-controlled subscription system.
