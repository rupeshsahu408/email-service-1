Create a new **post-auth success intro animation** for **Sendora** that appears after both:

1. successful login
2. successful signup

After the animation completes, automatically navigate the user to the **inbox/dashboard**.

## Core direction

The animation should feel:

* **cinematic**
* **bold**
* **dark luxury**
* **premium**
* **dramatic**
* **original to Sendora**

It should take inspiration from a **high-end streaming/cinematic brand feel**, but **must not copy Netflix directly**.
Also, **do not imitate Gmail, Google Workspace, or any Google-style motion/design**.

The result should feel like a strong branded intro moment for Sendora.

## Duration

* Total duration: **around 5 seconds**
* It should feel intentional and premium, not rushed
* Keep pacing smooth and elegant

## Visual style

Use a **dark cinematic background** with a **golden luxury atmosphere**.

### Background treatment

* Base background should be **near-black / deep black**
* Add a **soft blurred golden radial glow**
* The golden light should feel diffused, premium, and atmospheric
* Keep edges darker for a cinematic vignette effect
* Do **not** use a flat gold background
* Do **not** make it bright yellow
* The overall feeling should be dark, rich, and luxurious

Suggested palette direction:

* black / near-black base
* soft gold / amber glow
* subtle support from Sendora’s blue/purple tones where appropriate

## Logo reveal

Use the **Sendora logo** as the hero element.

### Important

* Keep the **original Sendora logo colors intact**
* Do **not** recolor the logo fully into gold
* Gold should feel like the surrounding cinematic lighting
* The logo itself should still look clearly like the Sendora brand

### Reveal behavior

The logo should:

* appear in a dramatic and premium way
* emerge from darkness
* feel crisp, luminous, and centered
* stay on screen long enough to feel memorable

Suggested motion:

* logo starts subtly hidden / dim
* scales in smoothly
* blooms with a premium glow on entry
* settles into a refined ambient glow
* includes a light sweep / highlight pass
* can include subtle expanding rings or ripple energy if done tastefully

Keep everything elegant and controlled.
Do not make it flashy, noisy, or game-like.

## Text

After the logo reveal, show:

**Welcome to Sendora**

This text should:

* appear below the logo
* fade in softly or rise gently from below
* feel cinematic and premium
* use elegant spacing and smooth timing

Optional secondary line below it:
**Your private inbox**

If used, keep it subtle and refined.

## Timing guidance

Use a premium cinematic sequence across ~5 seconds, for example:

* background atmosphere builds first
* logo reveal begins
* glow/light sweep enhances the logo
* logo holds as the hero moment
* “Welcome to Sendora” appears below
* scene exits smoothly into the inbox/dashboard

The logo should remain visible long enough to create a strong brand moment.

## Exit transition

After the intro:

* smoothly fade/transition into the inbox/dashboard
* no abrupt cut
* the transition should feel integrated with the product experience

## Functional requirements

* Trigger after **successful login**
* Trigger after **successful signup**
* Then redirect to **inbox/dashboard**
* Add **Skip** button or allow **Escape key** to skip immediately
* Respect **prefers-reduced-motion**

  * for reduced motion, show a simplified static/fade version briefly, then navigate
* Must work well on **mobile and desktop**
* Keep it **lightweight and performant**
* Avoid heavy effects that hurt loading/performance

## Design principles

* dark luxury
* cinematic reveal
* premium motion
* strong Sendora brand presence
* minimal but memorable
* original, not derivative

## Avoid

* copying Netflix branding directly
* copying Gmail or Google motion language
* overly bright yellow/gold backgrounds
* cheesy lens flares
* excessive particles
* cluttered visuals
* long loading-feel animation

## Goal

The final result should feel like a **signature Sendora success intro**:
premium, dramatic, branded, smooth, and memorable.
