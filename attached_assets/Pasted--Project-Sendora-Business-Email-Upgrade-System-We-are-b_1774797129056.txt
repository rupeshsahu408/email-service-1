## 🟣 Project: Sendora Business Email Upgrade System

We are building a **premium Business Email system** inside Sendora.

The goal is to provide:

* Custom domain emails
* Verified business identity (Golden Tick)
* Team email features
* Fast and trusted email experience

---

## 🎯 Core Concept

Sendora will have 2 user types:

### 1. Free Users

* Email format: `name@name.sendora.me`
* Limited storage
* No verification badge

### 2. Business Users (Paid via Razorpay)

* Custom domain email (e.g. `you@yourcompany.com`)
* Golden Verified Tick
* Multiple mailboxes
* Team access
* Higher storage
* No Sendora branding

---

## 💳 Payment Integration (Razorpay)

* Use Razorpay for subscription payments
* Monthly plan (initial)
* On successful payment:

  * Upgrade user to "business"
  * Set plan expiry (30 days)
* Handle via webhook:

  * payment.captured
  * subscription.charged

---

## 🟡 Golden Tick Verification System

Golden Tick is a **verified badge for business users only**

### Conditions:

* User must have active Business plan
* Domain must be verified
* Email must be active

### Display:

* Show golden tick icon next to email / sender name
* Visible in:

  * Inbox list
  * Email view
  * Profile section

---

## 🌐 Custom Domain Setup

Allow user to:

1. Add domain
2. Show DNS records (MX, TXT)
3. Verify domain
4. Activate email

Flow:

* Input domain
* Show DNS instructions
* Check verification
* Enable sending/receiving

---

## 👥 Team & Mailboxes

Business users can:

* Create multiple emails:

  * info@
  * support@
  * team@
* Add team members
* Assign roles:

  * Admin
  * Member

Permissions:

* Read emails
* Send emails
* Manage settings (admin only)
